The Beagle Blog

The Beagle is a breed of small scent hound ,similar in appearance to the much larger foxhound. The beaglewas developed primarily for hunting hare,known as beagling. Possessing a great sense of smell and superior tracking instincts , the beagle is the primarily breed used as a detection dog for prohibited agriculture imports and foodstufs in quarantine around the world. The beagle is intelligent and is a popular pet due to its size,good temper , and a lack of inherited health problems.
